# PrimeCare Platform Master List

## Admin
- **Dashboard**: Stats, Backups, System Settings.
- **Users**: RBAC Management, Role Assignments.
- **Audit**: Activity Logs, CSV Export.

## Manager (Operations)
- **Clients**: Admission, Care Plan Oversight.
- **Scheduling**: Shift Creation, PSW Assignment.
- **Review**: Daily Entry Approval, Incident Closing.

## RN (Clinical Supervisor)
- **Care Plans**: Creation, Risk Level Assessment.
- **Review**: Clinical Daily Entry Review.
- **Supervision**: PSW Performance Monitoring.

## PSW (Caregiver)
- **Today**: Visit Checklist, Check-in/out.
- **Entries**: ADL Checklist, Meds, Vitals.
- **Incidents**: Reporting at Point of Care.
